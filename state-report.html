00 \u00b7 How this comparison works
\n

One operator, measured against its own state jurisdiction.

\n

We place a telco's external cyber risk posture next to the real average of every analyzed operator in its state jurisdiction \u2014 same public, non-intrusive data on both sides. The composite score is a max-dominant blend of five weighted pillars \u2014 threat & exploit exposure, external attack surface, infrastructure risk, web application risk and email security \u2014 so a single severely compromised pillar cannot be masked by strength elsewhere. The operator's headline score is the highest composite across all of its distinct hosts. Alongside it, six individual exposures \u2014 Critical-band membership, CISA KEV, elevated EPSS, dangling DNS, weak DMARC and weak SPF \u2014 are each compared to the share of state peers carrying the same type of cyber risk.
